Risk Averse Portfolio Management

Establish Your Risk-Averse Portfolio

  • Risky Component: 30% of Porfolio Value - ETF(share based)
  • Riskless Component: 70% of Portolio Vaule - ETF(fixed income based)

If not registered, register for this type of portfolio with below button. Once registered just log-in with your user-name and password, the risk-averse portfolio type will automatically be attached to your profile and you will be alerted to adjust your portfolio if the portfolio value distribution deviates from your chosen 70% risky component/ 30% riskless component split.
